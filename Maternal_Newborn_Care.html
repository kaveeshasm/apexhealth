Maternal and Newborn Care

Apex Health Hospital is committed to providing comprehensive care for mothers and newborns throughout the pregnancy journey. Our Maternal and Newborn Care services ensure the safety, comfort, and well-being of both mother and baby, offering personalized care from prenatal visits to postnatal support. With a team of experienced obstetricians, pediatricians, and nurses, we ensure that both you and your baby receive the highest quality care during this special time.

Our Maternal and Newborn Care Services Include:


  • Regular check-ups, ultrasounds, and health screenings to monitor the health and development of both mother and baby.
  • Safe and comfortable labor and delivery services, including options for natural birth and cesarean sections, supported by skilled obstetricians and midwives.
  • Comprehensive care for both mother and baby after birth, including recovery monitoring, breastfeeding support, and newborn health checks.
  • Specialized care for premature or high-risk newborns, ensuring they receive the attention and treatment needed for a healthy start in life.
  • Classes and counseling on pregnancy, childbirth, and newborn care to help new parents feel confident and prepared.

Why Choose Apex Health Hospital for Maternal and Newborn Care?

  • Our team of doctors, nurses, and midwives are experts in maternal and neonatal care, providing compassionate and skilled support.
  • We offer tailored care plans to meet the unique needs of each mother and baby, ensuring a smooth pregnancy and delivery experience.
  • Our hospital is equipped with modern birthing suites, neonatal care units, and all the necessary resources to ensure safe, high-quality care.
  • We provide a nurturing, family-friendly environment where you and your baby can bond and thrive.
  • Our maternity team is available around the clock for urgent care, ensuring that you receive immediate attention when needed.

At Apex Health Hospital, we are dedicated to ensuring a safe, healthy, and positive experience for both mother and baby. From pregnancy to delivery and beyond, you can trust us to provide the care and support you need.
