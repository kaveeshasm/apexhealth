Services



Doctor Channeling

At Apex Health Hospital, we offer convenient doctor
channeling services, allowing patients to easily book
appointments with experienced specialists and general physicians.
Whether you need a routine check-up or consultation with a specialist,
our channeling service ensures timely access to expert medical care.
With flexible scheduling options, you can book an appointment in person or by phone.



Pharmacy

Apex Health Hospital provides a fully equipped on-site pharmacy,
offering a wide range of prescription medications, over-the-counter drugs,
and medical supplies. Our knowledgeable pharmacists are available to
offer expert advice and ensure you receive the correct medication promptly,
making it easier to manage your health conveniently under one roof.



Surgical Services

At Apex Health Hospital, we offer a wide range of advanced surgical
services performed by skilled surgeons and supported by state-of-the-art facilities.
From routine procedures to complex surgeries, our team ensures the highest
level of care, focusing on patient safety, comfort, and successful outcomes.



Cardiology Unit

Our Cardiology Unit at Apex Health Hospital is dedicated to providing
comprehensive heart care. With advanced diagnostic tools and a team of
experienced cardiologists, we offer a range of services, from preventive
heart health screenings to the treatment of complex cardiovascular conditions.
Our goal is to ensure the best possible care for your heart, promoting long-term wellness.



Maternal and Newborn Care

Apex Health Hospital is dedicated to supporting mothers and their
babies through every stage of pregnancy and beyond. Our Maternal
and Newborn Care services provide comprehensive prenatal care,
safe labor and delivery options, and postnatal support to ensure
the health and well-being of both mother and child. With our
experienced medical team, we are committed to making this special
journey safe and comfortable.



Dental Services

Apex Health Hospital offers comprehensive dental services to help you
maintain optimal oral health. Our experienced dental professionals
provide a range of treatments, including routine check-ups, cleanings,
fillings, and cosmetic procedures. We are committed to providing gentle,
effective care in a comfortable environment, ensuring your smile remains
bright and healthy.
