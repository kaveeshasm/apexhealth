Cardiology Unit

At Apex Health Hospital, our Cardiology Unit is dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and management of heart and vascular conditions. With a team of experienced cardiologists and state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, we offer comprehensive care for patients with a wide range of cardiovascular issues. From preventive screenings to complex treatments, our goal is to help you achieve and maintain optimal heart health.

Our Cardiology Services Include:


  • Personalized consultations to assess heart health and develop tailored treatment plans.
  • We offer advanced diagnostic tools, including ECGs, echocardiograms, and stress tests, to detect and monitor heart conditions.
  • Minimally invasive procedures such as angioplasty and stenting to treat coronary artery disease and other heart conditions.
  • Comprehensive care and treatment plans to manage and improve the quality of life for heart failure patients.
  • Screenings, lifestyle counseling, and treatments aimed at preventing heart disease and promoting heart health.

Why Choose Our Cardiology Unit?

  • Our team of highly trained specialists is committed to providing expert, compassionate care.
  • We use cutting-edge diagnostic and treatment tools to deliver precise and effective heart care.
  • From diagnosis to treatment and follow-up care, we provide a full range of cardiology services.
  • We tailor our care plans to each patient’s unique needs, ensuring the best outcomes for heart health.
  • Our cardiology team is available around the clock to handle urgent and emergency heart conditions.

At Apex Health Hospital’s Cardiology Unit, we are committed to helping you live a heart-healthy life with personalized care and advanced treatments. Your heart is in good hands with us.
